Professional Experience

Governance & Youth Development Consultant

For the past four years, Mwila has served as a Governance and Youth Development Consultant. In this capacity, he has collaborated with government officials, diplomatic dignitaries, international organisations, and regional bodies to drive impactful initiatives. His expertise has supported esteemed institutions such as the SADC Parliamentary Forum, Community of Democracies, COMESA Secretariat, Zambia's Ministry of Youth, Sport, and Arts, the Southern African Youth Forum, YouLead Afrika in East Africa, the Center for Young Leaders in Africa, Friedrich-Ebert-Stiftung Foundation (Zambian office), the U.S. Embassy in Zambia, and the Swedish Embassy in Zambia, among others.

Mwila's work through BeRelevant Afrika exemplifies a commitment to fostering credible leadership and sustainable youth development across the region and beyond.

BeRelevant Afrika - Founder (2020 - Present)

Mwila is the Founder of BeRelevant Afrika, a non-profit leadership development institute dedicated to shaping the leaders Africa needs to achieve the continent's aspirations. Under his leadership, the institute drives a transformative vision to equip individuals with 21st-century leadership skills and knowledge, foster dynamic platforms for engagement, and contribute to thought leadership through evidence-based research.

The BeRelevant Leadership Academy is our program of youth leadership development, since its inception in August 2022 the Academy has trained 1,700 young leaders. The Academy capacitates young leaders with the skills they need for the future they envision.

In partnership with the Japanese Embassy, we donated 300+ desks to 10 different schools in Chingola District in 2024. The official handover of the desks was done by Hon. Chipoka Mulenga the Minister of Commerce, Trade and Industry and the Japanese Ambassador to Zambia Mr. TAKEUCHI Kazuyuk

The Teens Leadership Program is BeRelevant's initiative to empower Zambian Teenagers with the necessary skills to be outstanding leaders in school, social life and in their career development endeavour. The program has so far trained 50 Zambian Teens.

Leadership Experience

CoD YouthLead for Zambia - Community of Democracy

The CoD YouthLeads is an informal, voluntary initiative comprising young pro-democracy advocates who provide strategic advice to the Member States of the Community of Democracies. Their mission is to enhance youth engagement and participation in democratic processes worldwide. As part of this team, Mwila offers actionable recommendations on governance and international affairs to the Governing Council, addressing critical issues affecting democracy.

In 2023, during the Governing Council meeting held in London, Mwila represented the YouthLeads and delivered a powerful keynote address as the Youth Speaker, showcasing the vital role of young voices in shaping democratic practices globally.

UNITED KINGDOM, LONDON MISSION: In 2023 Mwila was on an International Relations call of Duty as CoD YouthLead from Zambia to speak on the future of democracy and how Africa is positioning itself in strengthening the very fabric of democracy.

POLAND, WARSAW MISSION: In 2023, Mwila had an engagement mission in Warsaw, Poland and the cause of the meeting was to discuss on key global issues such as the Russia-Ukraine War, development of the Youth Democracy Network and Role of youth in electoral processes.

SOUTH KOREA, SEOUL MISSION: In 2024, Mwila attended the 3RD SUMMIT FOR DEMOCRACY in Seoul as one of his missions, during the visit Mwila had the opportunity to engage with key democracy advocates around the world, and gave a speech on Youth Electoral Participation.

Commonwealth National Youth Representative for Zambia (2021 – 2023)

Mwila was competitively selected by the National Youth Development Council of Zambia to serve as the National Youth Delegate, representing all Zambian youths in the Commonwealth region. In this esteemed capacity, he officially represented the country at the 2022 Commonwealth Youth Forum and the Commonwealth Heads of Government Summit held in Kigali, Rwanda.

UNILUS Student Union President (2016 – 2017)

While pursuing his undergraduate degree in Politics and International Relations, Mwila served as President of the University of Lusaka Student Union, where his leadership focused on driving meaningful change. He championed the reduction of student fees, established systems to ensure the full inclusion of students living with disabilities, and actively participated in programs addressing mental health challenges and drug addiction among students. Following his tenure, Mwila's outstanding leadership was formally recognized by the university management.

Recognition & Awards

Governance and Civic Leadership Award (2020)

Mwila was honored with the Governance and Civic Leadership Award by the Ministry of Youth, Sports, and Child Development, in partnership with the National Youth Development Council. This recognition celebrated his unwavering dedication to youth development, his selfless contributions to his community, and his distinguished representation of Zambia on national and international platforms. Additionally, Mwila was named among Zambia's Top 35 Youth Leaders Under 35, further cementing his status as a transformative leader.

Best Non-Fiction Author of the Year 2018

Mwila received the prestigious Best Non-Fiction Award of the Year from the CDI National Book Awards for his impactful Pan-African book, I AM AFRICA. This thought-provoking work advocates for Africa's transformation by championing key themes such as patriotism, intra-African trade, silencing the guns, cultural identity, and industrial revolution. The book stands as a rallying call for a united and progressive Africa.

Published Works

Mwila is the celebrated author of three best-selling books that boldly challenge the status quo of development, inspiring readers to envision and create a society that reflects the values of equity, progress, and unity for both the nation and the continent.

I AM AFRICA (2018)

Officially launched at the University of Lusaka with Guest of Honor Dr Vernon Johnson Mwaanga, the Freedom Fighter. The book has sold more than 1,000 copies nationwide and across the region.

DE-CORONA-ZATION (2020)

Published during COVID-19 Pandemic, providing researched suggestions and ideas on how nations can build back better in Post-COVID-19. The book received national recognition as a key publication at such a pressing time.

GIANTS OF LIBERATION (2023)

Officially launched at Mulungushi Conference Center by the former First Lady of Zambia Mrs. Esther Lungu. The first Zambian book that profiles the galant leaders that fought for Zambia's liberation, written for future generations.
